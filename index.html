About SmartReception

SmartReception transforms traditional visitor management with a secure, cloud-based solution that enhances security while improving the visitor experience. Our system replaces paper logs with digital registration that's both faster and more secure.

Designed for facilities of all sizes, SmartReception provides enterprise-grade features with intuitive interfaces that require minimal training. All visitor data is encrypted and stored in compliance with global privacy regulations.

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can we implement SmartReception?

Most organizations complete setup in under 48 hours. Our team provides onboarding assistance and your reception staff can be trained in less than an hour.

What hardware do we need?

SmartReception works with any modern tablet or computer. We recommend iPad devices for optimal experience, but any device with a modern browser will work.

Is visitor data secure?

Yes. All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest. We comply with GDPR, CCPA, and ISO 27001 standards. Regular security audits ensure continuous protection.
